PHOTOGRAPHY


Story behind each photograph is different. It potrays some different story, value and perception. But it is upto us to understand the story.

Location: Igatpuri
Day:14th November, 2021
Time: 11.50 am

The beautiful scenery that we can see is from the Tringalwadi Dam.
Igatpuri, a place of beautiful mountains, hills and fresh air. Staying in a resort amid the mountains where you can see the sun rise, foggy mountains and hear the birds sing.
What more is needed you might be thinking. But yes, being a traveller you are always wanting to travel and explore the place.

Finding the place wasn't that easy. Even though we had a google map it directed us through roads that were uneven and had not been built properly. The road wasn't easy to travel, we started losing hope. But after coming so close to the destination according to the google maps, we said to ourselves let's take a leap of faith and continue our way ahead. And there we were 'The Tringalwadi Dam'. Beautiful lush green mountains and this beautiful lake in front of us.
